Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder with ADHD and a TBI survivor who was suffering from chronic migraines. Struggling to maintain healthy exercise habits, he turned to what he knew worked: the immersive world of video games. By merging video games and exercise, he not only improved his health but also his reaction time, brain-body relationship, and gaming skills. GamerXise is now a solution for gamers worldwide, offering full-body workouts that can be done right from your living room, with no additional equipment required.
